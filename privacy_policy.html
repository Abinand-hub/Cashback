  2. Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

We at Cashback (“we,” “us,” or “our”) are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and secure information when you access our website, documentation, smart contracts, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

2. Information We Collect

  • Public Data: Wallet addresses, blockchain transaction data, and token balances.
  • Account Data: Email address, username (if applicable).
  • Usage Data: Events related to cashback activities and feature usage.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device type, timestamps.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and maintain the Services.
  • Provide and track cashback rewards.
  • Improve security and performance.
  • Communicate updates and respond to inquiries.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share aggregated or anonymized data.
  • We may disclose information if legally required.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to analyze usage and improve the user experience. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as needed for service purposes, legal obligations, and enforcement.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local authority.

8. Security

We apply appropriate security safeguards. However, no system is completely secure. You use our Services at your own risk.

9. International Transfers

Data may be transferred across borders. By using our Services, you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted with an updated effective date.

11. Contact

Email us at Cashbackerc20@gmail.com for privacy-related inquiries.
