Revolutionizing Transaction Privacy and Efficiency

Traditional Ethereum transactions pass through the public mempool, exposing them to risks like frontrunning. But there’s a better way: direct-to-builder routing.

Benefits of Private Routing

  • Increased Privacy: Transactions remain hidden until included in a block.
  • Front-Running Protection: Prevents malicious bots from exploiting your trades.
  • Faster Confirmation: Builders can prioritize value-packed blocks more effectively.

Each transaction contributes to a shared profit pool, fairly distributed using cryptographic systems like Merkle Trees. It’s decentralized profit-sharing at its best.
